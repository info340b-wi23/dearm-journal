Dream Journal Project Proposal

Problem Description

The problem is that people have a hard time remember their dreams. In order to get a better understanding of themselves, remembering and paying more attention to their dreams is important.

Many people are not able to recall their dreams or they are only able to remember small pieces of it. When someone dreams, it is a series of images, thoughts, and sensations that occur in the mind during sleep. Dreaming is a very important step in sleeping that many people may overlook. Dreams can also provide insight into unconscious thoughts, feelings, and desire. For someone to truly understand themselves, they must understand what they are dreaming about and how that may have an impact on your life. Studies have also shown that people who dream about a task they have learned, perform better on that task the next day. Also keeping a dream journal can improve memory by helping to solidify dream memories and make them more accessible. Writing down dreams immediately upon waking can help to preserve the details and emotions of the dream before they fade away. The act of writing can also help to process the dream and connect it to waking life experiences, so being able to remember your dreams and understand them can ultimately help you get a better understanding of certain aspects of life in the real world. This is why the Dream Journal app would be very beneficial for a lot of people.

One existing attempt we found is called Wakefully, this app provides the feature of letting users put down their dream and helping them unlock the dream by interpreting the meaning behind. We also have this resource that helps us identify our problem domain and talked about the Power of a Dream Journal

Application Description

What is a Dream Journal?

Dream Journal is made out of the purpose of helping people remember and understand their dream. Therefore, anyone that is interested in recording their dreams and wants to get more sense of themselves could be our users. Certainly, it is also very suitable for people with higher demands for recording their dreams. For example, those with bad memories or those who suffer a lot from nightmares. Plus, the rich dream contents over our platform could also attracts people that need creative inspiration or psychologies that have the needs to study dreams. Users can view the dream information from themselves or from the "community" if they choose to share their dreams or reports to others. After using our app and getting a place to organize their dreams, users won't encounter the situation of having a hard time recalling their dreams. Moreover, they will receive their personalized dream report to help understand themselves better.

Journal the Dream

On our web app, users are able to jot down dreams into entries. By putting in specific dates, a visible calendar will be presented for them to get an overview of the dreams they have recorded. In terms of the content in each dream entry, users will be asked to put in the type, quality, duration, moods, and elements of that dream as well as the specific story they dreamed of.

A Dream Community

Users have the chance to interact with others by scrolling through the community page that others will share their dreams at. If desired, they could also publicize their dreams there.

Dream Reports

We will provide users with a dream report by organizing the dreams in their entries. Specifically, telling users the overall quality of their dream, most frequent type of dream, or most occurring elements in their dream.
